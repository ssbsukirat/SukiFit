# Methionine

Methionine is an essential amino acid the human body cannot produce on its own, hence, it is required to be ingested through nutrition for optimal health.

## Average recommended intake
- 1.05g (0.15g/kg)

## Food sources
- egg;
- fish;
- seaweed;
- soy;
- seeds;
- nuts;
- cheese;
- lamb;
- beef;
- pork.
