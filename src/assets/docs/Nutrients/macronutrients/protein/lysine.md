# Lysine

Lysine is an essential amino acid the human body cannot produce on its own, hence, it is required to be ingested through nutrition for optimal health.

## Average recommended intake
- 2.1g/day (0.3g/kg)

## Food sources
- egg white;
- soy;
- cheese;
- beef;
- pork.
