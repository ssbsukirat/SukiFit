# Leucine

Leucine is an essential amino acid the human body cannot produce on its own, hence, it is required to be ingested through nutrition for optimal health.

## Average recommended intake
- 2.8g/day (0.4g/kg)

## Food sources
- egg white;
- soy;
- seaweed;
- fish;
- seeds;
- dairy;
- tofu.
