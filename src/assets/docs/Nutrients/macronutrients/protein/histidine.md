# Histidine

Histidine is an essential amino acid the human body cannot produce on its own, hence, it is required to be ingested through nutrition for optimal health.

## Average recommended intake
- 0.7g/day (0.1g/kg);

## Food sources
- soy;
- egg white;
- cheese;
- seeds;
- beef;
- tofu;
- pork
